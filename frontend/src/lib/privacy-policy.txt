Welcome to Insight Engine ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

A. Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name and email address. This is necessary to create and manage your account.
Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, our payment processor, Stripe, will collect your payment card information. We do not store or have direct access to your full payment card details.
Content You Submit: We collect the content you provide for analysis. This includes:
Audio Files: Any audio files you upload for transcription.
Text Content: Any text, such as transcripts, that you paste into our service.
Analysis Results: We store the resulting analyses, summaries, threads, and other content generated by our engine for you to access from your dashboard.
B. Information We Collect Automatically

Log and Usage Data: Like most websites, our servers automatically collect information when you access our service. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the dates and times of your access. We use this information for security, debugging, and to maintain our service.
Cookies: We use cookies to maintain your user session. A cookie is a small file placed on your device that allows us to keep you logged in as you navigate our site. Our use of cookies is limited to what is functionally necessary for the service to work.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To provide, operate, and maintain our service.
To process your audio and text content through our transcription and analysis engines.
To create and manage your account and authenticate you as a user.
To process payments and subscriptions.
To communicate with you, including sending important service updates or responding to your support requests.
To monitor for security threats and prevent fraudulent activity.
3. Who We Share Your Information With (Third-Party Services)
We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information with the following third-party service providers (sub-processors) who help us operate our service. They are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for the specific services they provide to us.

Google (Firebase & Google AI Platform): We use Google's services for multiple core functions. Firebase is used for user authentication, database hosting, and file storage (your uploaded content and analyses). We use Google's Gemini AI models to perform the core analysis of your text content. 4. Data Retention and Deletion
We believe you should have control over your content.

Content Retention: We store your uploaded files and generated analyses for as long as you choose to keep them in your account. You can delete individual analysis jobs at any time from your dashboard.
Deletion: When you delete a specific analysis job, it is immediately and permanently removed from our active databases.
Account Deletion: If you choose to delete your account via the Settings page, all of your personal information (name, email) and all associated content (uploaded files, analyses) will be permanently deleted from our systems.
5. Your Data Rights and Choices
You have rights concerning your personal information, including:

The right to Access: You can access your personal data and analysis history at any time through your account's Settings and Dashboard pages.
The right to Correction: You can update your account information, such as your name, in the Settings page.
The right to Deletion: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data, as described in the section above.
6. Data Security
We take the security of your data seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect it. This includes using industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transit and relying on the robust security infrastructure of our partners like Google, Stripe, and AssemblyAI.

7. Children's Privacy
Our service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@insightengine.com
